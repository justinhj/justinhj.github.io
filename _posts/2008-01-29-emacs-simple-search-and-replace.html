--- layout: post title: emacs - a simple search and replace function date: '2008-01-29T14:42:00.000-08:00' author: Justin tags: [emacs] modified_time: '2008-01-29T14:43:41.320-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4777243148323391813.post-2382190268593974630 blogger_orig_url: http://justinsboringpage.blogspot.com/2008/01/emacs-simple-search-and-replace.html --- This function is for emacs; it searchs for any sequence composed of spaces and tabs and replaces it with a single tab


(defun space-and-tabs-to-tab()
  (interactive)
  (while (re-search-forward "[\t ]+" nil t)
    (replace-match "\t" nil nil)))
