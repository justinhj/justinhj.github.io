--- layout: post title: Linking MingW32 with psapi date: '2009-05-14T16:48:00.000-07:00' author: Justin tags: - mingw32 link errors GetProcessMemoryInfo EnumProcesses modified_time: '2009-05-14T16:51:25.869-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4777243148323391813.post-5144824078220087756 blogger_orig_url: http://justinsboringpage.blogspot.com/2009/05/linking-mingw32-with-psapi.html --- Here's something I came across that didn't have a good answer when I googled. If you get link errors with MingW32 like:

undefined reference to `GetProcessMemoryInfo@12' or
c:/justinhj/mem.cpp:55: undefined reference to `EnumProcesses@12'

Then you are not linking with the library psapi, and you need it.

To do this just add -lpsapi to your command line. It needs to be last in the list of libraries too!
