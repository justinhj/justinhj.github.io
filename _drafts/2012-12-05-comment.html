Virtualized development tool. Easily create development environments declaratively and launch them in local VM. Uses Oracle’s Virtual box for the VM’s and Puppet for the declarative environment.

Via this system you can quickly switch between saved copies of virtual development environments, knowing that your environment matches exactly the production environment and those of other developers.
