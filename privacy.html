Privacy

Privacy and Security Notice

I respect your privacy and am committed to providing a secure browsing experience. Here is how this site protects your data:

1. Secure Connection (HTTPS)

This website is served entirely over HTTPS. This means the connection between your browser and the servers hosting this site is fully encrypted.

  • Protection from Snooping: Your internet service provider, or anyone else on your network, cannot intercept or manipulate the data you are viewing on this site.
  • Integrity: HTTPS ensures that the pages you receive are exactly what I published, without any unauthorized modifications or injected advertisements along the way.

2. Analytics and Tracking

To help improve this website and understand how it performs, I use Cloudflare Web Analytics. This service provides basic website traffic metrics without compromising your privacy.

  • No Cookies or Local Storage: This site does not use cookies or local storage to track your behavior across the internet.
  • No Personal Data Collected: The analytics service does not collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
  • No Fingerprinting: Your visits are not tracked by fingerprinting your IP address, User Agent string, or any other device-specific data.

The data collected is completely anonymous and limited to high-level technical metrics, such as page views, load times, general geographic regions, and browser types. This simply helps me ensure the site is running smoothly and see which content is most useful.
